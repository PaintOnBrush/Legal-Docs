The CSX railroad, like other Class One railroads has been phasing out signal control circuits on pole line for many years.  Initially, many went to the use of cable instead of open line wire, and then to electronic track circuits with the signals control by codes in the rails, or the use of radio for code lines to control points.  I believe that CSX has almost entirely completed this phase-out.  I will however, check with our Southeastern area office to confirm the number, if any, of miles and locations left.
