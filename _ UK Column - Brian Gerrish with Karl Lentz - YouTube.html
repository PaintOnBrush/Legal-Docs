Published on Dec 18, 2013

This is the final hour extracted from the UK Column Live broadcast of 17th November 2013.

Some very important information which goes some way to explaining how the legal system works against us. and what we can do about it.

While the information given is of great value it must be stressed this is not a quick-fix "How To" video. You'll need further knowledge to be able to present and win your case in court. This may be of particular interest and indeed, usefulness, to those involved in the secret Family Court hearings.

This edit was done and uploaded without consultation with, or permission from, UK Column editors or producers. I beg their indulgence.

Much Love to Paul and Asha and all who have given and are giving them support.
Special thanks to Jane and Matthew.
